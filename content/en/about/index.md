**Dinozorlarla Kafa Ütüleme (DKU)** is a Turkish-language technology podcast hosted by two experienced software developers who have been in the industry since the 90s.

## Hosts

### Alper Konuralp

Alper is a Solution Architect at Doğuş Teknoloji with deep expertise in software architecture, enterprise systems, and modern development practices.

### Burak Selim Şenyurt

Burak is an experienced software developer with a passion for programming languages, system design, and the philosophical dimensions of software engineering.
